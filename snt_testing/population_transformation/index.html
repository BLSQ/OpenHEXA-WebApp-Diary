Pipeline

A.5 DHIS2 Population Transformation

Processes DHIS2 population data: scaling, projections, and disaggregation by demographic group.

Parameters
Part 1 — Scaling
Population reference
Total population for scaling DHIS2 data proportionally.
Population year reference
Year matching the population reference.
Part 2 — Disaggregation
Proportion population under 5
Proportion of population aged under 5.
Proportion population pregnant women
Proportion of pregnant women population.
Proportion population 0–1 years
Proportion of population aged 0–1.
Proportion population 1–2 years
Proportion of population aged 1–2.
Proportion population 5–10 years
Proportion of population aged 5–10.
Proportion population 5–36 months
Proportion of population aged 5–36 months.
Use disaggregation proportions (.csv)
Path to a CSV file with population disaggregations at ADM2 level already uploaded to the workspace bucket (e.g. /data/disaggregation.csv). Leave blank to skip.
Part 3 — Projections
Projection growth rate required
Annual growth rate for projecting population figures (e.g. 0.025 for 2.5%).
Projection reference year required
Base year for population projections.
